Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services, processes, and pricing.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Basic graphic designs can be completed within 24-48 hours, while comprehensive branding or video production projects may take several weeks. We provide a detailed timeline with every quote.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! While we have standard packages, we specialize in tailoring solutions to meet your unique needs and budget. Contact us for a personalized consultation and quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including M-Pesa, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards. Payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before project commencement, with clear milestones for larger projects.

How do I get started with a project?

It's simple! Reach out to us via our contact form, email, or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, requirements, and provide a comprehensive proposal and quote tailored to your needs.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Yes, certainly! Please visit our Portfolio page to see a diverse range of our completed projects across various industries. We're proud of our work!

What is your revision policy?

Our packages typically include a set number of revisions to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions beyond the package limit may incur extra charges, which will be communicated transparently.

Do you offer ongoing support or maintenance?

For certain services, like full website creation, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages. This ensures your digital assets remain up-to-date, secure, and performing optimally. Details can be discussed during your consultation.

How do you handle client communication during a project?

We believe in clear and consistent communication. We use a combination of email, phone calls, and dedicated project management tools to keep you updated on progress, gather feedback, and address any questions promptly.
