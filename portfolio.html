Project Spotlight: Caphy

We are actively working on 'Caphy,' an innovative project aimed at elevating the phone camera experience. Caphy seeks to empower smartphone cameras to capture professional-quality pictures, comparable to standard cameras. We are developing a third-party camera app/system that can be finely regulated for optimal picture performance. Furthermore, Caphy will incorporate advanced AI capabilities to assist with perfect picture editing, mimicking the precision and artistry of a professional photo editor.
