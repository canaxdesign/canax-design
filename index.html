Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about our services.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Basic graphic designs can be completed within 24-48 hours, while comprehensive branding or video production projects may take several weeks. We provide a detailed timeline with every quote.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! While we have standard packages, we specialize in tailoring solutions to meet your unique needs and budget. Contact us for a personalized consultation and quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including M-Pesa, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards. Payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before project commencement.

How do I get started with a project?

It's simple! Reach out to us via our contact form, email, or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, requirements, and provide a comprehensive proposal.
